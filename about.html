Interior Backchannel is an event platform rooted in the underground electronic music scene of British Columbia's Interior. Built from a desire to define, and find the region's emerging sound.

The project exists to amplify artists who operate outside the mainstream and to give space to the eclectic sound. Focusing on raw, niche electronic genres, Interior Backchannel is dedicated to showcasing regional talent through carefully curated events.

Each experience is curated to feel intentional, immersive, and disparate compared to traditional nightlife. We prioritize sound, atmosphere, and individuality over convention.

More than just events, Interior Backchannel is an evolving creative outlet. It aims to present underground music in a new way.
